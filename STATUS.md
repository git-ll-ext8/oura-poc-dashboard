## Latest: undid the "live/syncing" framing — it was misleading, here's why

Good question you asked: "when does Activity/Steps actually populate?" Answer that exposed a real mistake in the last two changes — **this dashboard only checks Oura's servers once a day** (the cron job, or when someone signs in). There's no continuous checking in between. So "Syncing" was the wrong word — it suggested something resolving any minute, when really nothing changes again until tomorrow's pull. On top of that, Oura's own daily window for Activity closes at 4am the next morning, so even that one daily check almost never finds a finished score for "today" specifically.

**Net effect of the mistake:** for nearly the whole day, every day, the card would have sat on "Syncing" / 0 steps — which is exactly what you noticed.

**Fixed now:** back to showing the most recent day that has a real, finished number (both Activity and Steps together, from the same day) — with a small note when that's not today, e.g. "Activity/Steps as of Jul 19." The existing "Last updated" line is the one honest freshness signal now, instead of two signals that disagreed with each other.

**Worth deciding, no rush:** if you want Activity/Steps to actually update more than once a day, that needs either (a) Oura's "push" feature so updates arrive within ~30 seconds of someone syncing their ring — free, but more building, or (b) paying for Vercel's Pro plan (~$20/mo) to check every few hours instead of once a day — simpler to add, still not instant. Once-a-day is completely fine for the Thursday demo; this is only relevant if you want fresher numbers for ongoing day-to-day use afterward.

## Notes for the other agent (Codex fallback, `..\520.Codex`)

### 2026-07-21 (yet later): reverted the "live/syncing" framing — superseded by a correct understanding of our own polling cadence
- **Trigger**: Lawrence asked a simple, sharp question — "when will Activity/Steps actually populate? For most of the day it just shows Syncing and 0." That question is what surfaced the flaw; the two commits just before this one (`77a8749` "match the Oura app," `d04cd7c` "surface still syncing on main leaderboard") were built on a false premise.
- **The false premise**: those commits assumed treating Activity/Steps as "live" was honest because Oura's OWN app shows a live-updating value throughout the day. True for the real app — but **irrelevant for us**, because this dashboard's data pipeline (`lib/oura-sync.ts` → `syncMemberScores`) is only ever invoked from two places: the OAuth sign-in callback, or the once-daily cron (`app/api/cron/refresh-oura/route.ts`, `vercel.json` schedule `0 15 * * *`). There is no polling loop, no webhook receiver, nothing else that talks to Oura in between. So whatever "today's" activity/steps were AT THE MOMENT of that one daily pull is what stays displayed for the next ~24 hours, no matter how much real activity data piles up on Oura's own servers in the meantime. Calling that "live" or "syncing" was simply wrong given our actual architecture — nothing resolves again until tomorrow's pull, not "any minute now."

### Same-session follow-up: safety-hardened the refresh swap (supersedes parts of the entry below)
Prompted by an explicit instruction: Tracy's and Nadia's tokens must be treated as irreplaceable while Lawrence is on vacation, since he won't be available to help them re-sign-in if something goes wrong. Two changes:
